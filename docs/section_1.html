STATEMENT FROM SUPERINTENDENT

DAN PARENT

Danbury Local Schools is an excellent school district that provides
exceptional opportunities for students both in and outside of the classroom.
The district offers a wide range of courses and extracurricular options
that ensure every student is not only college or career ready, but they are
prepared to succeed in life after graduation.
It is with this deeper sense of purpose in mind that we present to you our
ﬁrst Quality Proﬁle. This is an honest look at the way Danbury Local Schools
operates and how we educate students in a more meaningful and complete
way. For far too many years Danbury has been the “best kept secret” in
Northwest Ohio.
Danbury is small enough to ensure every student gets the type of personal
attention they deserve from caring and devoted teachers. Yet we are also
providing amazing opportunities such as earning college credits while still
in high school, high levels of participation in athletics, visual and performing
arts as well as clubs and service organizations.
After reviewing this information I’m sure you will discover why Danbury
Local Schools has everything your child needs or wants to be successful
in school and in life. Should you have any questions about the amazing
opportunities that Danbury Local Schools has to offer please feel free to
contact us at any time.
Sincerely,
Dan Parent
Superintendent, Danbury Local School District

ACADEMICS
FINANCES
Danbury Local Schools understands that communication with parents and the
community is a necessary part of any successful partnership. As such the district
has developed a number of tools to keep parents and residents connected to what’s
happening in the schools. From emails to social media and lots of face-to-face
opportunities, our families know what’s happening in our schools.
COMMUNICATIONS
TECHNOLOGY
Today students must be able to master
more than basic facts. Students must
also be able to use the latest technology
to research information and then
communicate it with those around the
corner or around the globe. Danbury
Local Schools takes this need seriously
and provides students with a plethora of
opportunities to experience technology
as a part of their overall education.
This includes individual computers for
students in grades seven through 12 and
learning labs for students in younger
grades.
The classroom a student learns within is
just as important as the tools they use in
supporting their learning goals. Without
the right environment, students can’t
learn. This is why Danbury Local Schools
has maintained excellent facilities that
meet modern learning requirements.
From an upgraded technology
infrastructure to enhanced security
systems, Danbury Local Schools is able to
provide students and parents with the kind
of learning environment that allows every
student to safely and fully engage in their
educational journey.
LEARNING ENVIRONMENTS
WHOLE CHILD
Education today is more than the basic
facts a student learns while sitting in a
standard classroom. At Danbury Local
Schools we believe in educating the whole
child. This includes a variety of hands-
on experiences both in and outside of
the classroom. Danbury Local Schools
offers a wide variety of opportunities for
students to develop important skills such
as teamwork, leadership and setting goals.
From athletic teams, music programs
and the arts, Danbury Local Schools truly
offers a complete educational experience.
143 out of 163 students
88% of High School students participated
in extra curricular or Co curricular
activities during the 2015-16 school year.

88%

Another key component to the value a school district provides is how much does it cost.
Danbury Local Schools is effective and efﬁcient in our day-to-day operations. We ensure that
students have what they need to be successful while also holding the line on expenses. The
district hasn’t asked residents for new operating dollars in 23 years. Danbury Local Schools
has the second lowest property tax rate of the 39 school districts in Northwest Ohio.
The quality of education in a school district
is the cornerstone of the value it provides
the community. Danbury Local Schools
is proud to have an excellent quality
education that ranks among the top school
districts in Ohio. In addition, U.S. News
and World Report rated the district as one
of
America’s Best High Schools
for three
consecutive years. Students who attend
Danbury Local Schools receive the personal
attention they need to be successful in the
classroom and are well prepared for college
or the modern workforce after graduation.
